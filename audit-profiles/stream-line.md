# Stream Line - Audit Profile

## What This App Is
A free Slack-based business operations tool for live streamers who sell products during streams. It tracks sales, calculates profit/loss with real platform fees, manages inventory, tracks customers, and provides AI insights.

## What This App Is NOT
- NOT a streaming overlay tool (that's StreamElements/Streamlabs)
- NOT a chat bot for viewer engagement
- NOT a broadcasting tool
- NOT a content creation platform
- It does NOT handle live stream production, alerts, or viewer interaction

## Target Audience
- Live sellers on Whatnot, TikTok Shop, eBay Live
- Trading card breakers who sell during streams
- Resellers and flippers who use live streaming to sell inventory
- Small teams (1-5 people) running live sales operations
- Content creators who sell merch during streams

## The Problem We Solve
Streamers who sell products have no simple way to track their business. They use spreadsheets, mental math, or nothing. They don't know their real profit after platform fees. They lose track of inventory. They can't see which streams or products are actually profitable.

## Correct Competitors (tools that solve the SAME problem)
- Google Sheets / Excel (free but manual, no automation)
- QuickBooks ($30/month, too complex for solo streamers)
- Wave Accounting (free but not designed for per-stream tracking)
- No direct Slack-based competitor exists for this specific use case

## Incorrect Competitors (do NOT compare against these)
- StreamElements (streaming overlays and chat, not business tracking)
- Streamlabs (OBS integration and alerts, not P&L tracking)  
- Whatnot itself (marketplace, not analytics tool)
- Shopify (e-commerce platform, not stream-specific)

## Core Features
1. Log stream sessions with sales, costs, duration, platform
2. Automatic platform fee calculations (Whatnot 19.9%, TikTok 8%, etc.)
3. Real P&L per stream with cost of goods
4. Inventory tracking with velocity and reorder alerts
5. Customer database with VIP detection
6. Revenue goals with progress tracking
7. AI-powered business insights (best day, trends, recommendations)
8. Brand vault and AI content generator
9. CSV export for accountants
10. Multi-tenant (teams can share one workspace)

## UX Design
- Slack App Home with tab navigation (Dashboard, Insights, Tools, Settings)
- Everything is button-driven, no slash commands shown in UI
- Modals for all data entry
- Tips and Industry Workflows buttons always visible
- Status bar at top shows today's metrics

## Known Limitations
- Manual stream logging (unless API key connected)
- No direct Whatnot API integration yet (planned)
- Mobile Slack experience is functional but not optimized
